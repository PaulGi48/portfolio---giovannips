IMPORTANT INFORMATION

MEMO ON FINAL YEAR WEEK AND NYSC ORIENTATION

This is addressed to the final year students regarding their final year weekly activities dues and NYSC orientation. All final year students are expected to pay the sum of 45,000( forty five thousand naira only) for their final year week activities dues and the sum of 50,000(fifty thousand naira only) for final year souvenir.

Anyone who defaults in this payment won’t be allowed to write their final exams. Payments are to be made on or before the 5th of June ,2022.
